about

Nicotine is an advanced, so-called "ghost client" for Minecraft version 1.8.9, based on Minecraft Forge. Nicotine is made for the purpose of giving yourself various utilities, which all give you an advantage without anyone noticing it, including many render-related modules.

WARNING: Use this client at your own risk. Ghost clients are not fully undetectable. The Nicotine development team is not responsible for any damage you cause to your Minecraft accounts.
